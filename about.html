Capturing the Best of You through my lens.

LOS ANGELES BASED PHOTOGRAPHER

Andy Warhol once said, "The idea is not to live forever, but to create something that will." For me, that is photography. It captures the emotion of a single moment in time, allowing it to live on in our minds and memories. Photographs allow us to share and to communicate, which is why I want to share my message, stories, and ideas, with EACH OF YOU.

ABOUT ME

My name is T.y. Li, and I am originally from Hong Kong. I graduated from UCLA in 2017 with a Sociology major and a Film minor. I started photography as a hobby, but I fell in love with the way photography captured moments and translated powerful messages. I am fascinated by the talented individuals in the city, and I am inspired by you. I am blessed to live in Los Angeles with 4 million unique stories waiting to be told.
